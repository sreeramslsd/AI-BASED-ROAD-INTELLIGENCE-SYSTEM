Many students have great ideas but often miss events because they don’t get the information on time. Even when they know what to say, some hesitate to speak in front of others because they’re nervous or unsure. During a group discussion, some students told us they knew exactly what to speak but were too scared. When we asked them to write it down instead, they did it confidently.
